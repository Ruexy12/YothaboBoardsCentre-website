WHAT WE OFFER,

    1. 3D Design & Interior Design and Fittings

    •3D Design: Offers clients a realistic preview of spaces and furniture setups, helping them visualize and customize designs before installation.

    • Interior Design and Fittings: Provides comprehensive interior design services to create aesthetically pleasing and functional spaces, tailored to each client’s taste.

     2. Boards Cutting & Edging , Hardware Material and Window Blinds :All at fix and supply

    • Boards Cutting and Edging: Delivers precise board cutting and edging services to ensure a perfect fit for all cabinetry and furniture needs.

    • Hardware Material and Window Blinds: Supplies high-quality hardware materials and window blinds, along with installation services for a polished, functional finish.

     3. Bedroom Fittings & Kitchen Fittings

    • Bedroom Fittings: Specializes in creating stylish and organized bedroom setups, including wardrobes, shelves, and customized storage solutions.

    • Kitchen Fittings: Provides efficient kitchen fitting services, including cabinetry, countertops, and storage solutions to enhance both functionality and style.

     4. PVC Ceilings and Partitions & Wrapped Doors

    • PVC Ceilings and Partitions: Installs PVC ceilings and partitions that are durable, low-maintenance, and enhance the aesthetic appeal of any space.

    • Wrapped Doors: Offers wrapped doors in a variety of finishes, providing a modern look and durability, ideal for residential or commercial spaces.

     5. Office Cupboards & Shop Compartments

    • Office Cupboards: Designs and installs office cupboards to maximize space and organization, tailored to meet corporate needs.

    • Shop Compartments:Builds custom shop compartments, ensuring a well-organized and visually appealing display space for retail environments.
