What happens after we deliver the quotation?

After Yothabo receives a customer’s online quotation request, the team reviews the details, verifies pricing and availability, and prepares a detailed, itemized quotation. This quotation is then sent to the customer for review and approval. Once approved, the customer completes payment, and Yothabo confirms the order, providing delivery details. The team then processes, quality-checks, and dispatches the order, notifying the customer of its progress. Finally, after delivery, Yothabo follows up to ensure the customer is satisfied, completing a seamless experience from quotation to fulfillment.

MDF Superwood

• Superior Quality MDF Boards: MDF Superwood is ideal for a range of furniture and cabinet applications. We supply and work with this premium material known for its smooth finish, durability, and versatility. Perfect for custom cabinetry and shelving projects, MDF Superwood adds a high-end touch to any project.

High Gloss Boards

• Sleek, High-Shine Finishes: Add a touch of luxury with our high gloss boards, perfect for modern kitchens, wardrobes, and cabinetry. These boards offer a stunning reflective finish that’s easy to clean and maintain, providing a sleek and stylish upgrade to any room.

White and Colour Melamine

• Versatile and Vibrant Boards: Our melamine boards, available in classic white and a wide range of colors, are ideal for cabinetry, shelving, and furniture. Known for their durability and ease of maintenance, melamine boards provide a clean, contemporary look and are an excellent choice for any interior project.
